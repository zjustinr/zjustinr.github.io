About

Professor Z. Justin Ren is the Associate Professor and Department Chair of Operations and Technology Management at Boston University Questrom School of Business. He is also a Faculty Affiliate with the Boston University Institute for Global Sustainability. With expertise spanning business analytics, manufacturing operations, supply chain management, and clean energy systems, Prof. Ren has made significant contributions to academic research and industry practice.

His research and consulting work have impacted leading organizations including the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL), Bloomberg Philanthropy, Best Buy, Staples, and Intel. Prof. Ren is the author of four books and has published 26 papers in top-tier journals including Management Science, Operations Research, and the California Management Review.
